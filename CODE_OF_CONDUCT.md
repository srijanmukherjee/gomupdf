# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1, as its code of conduct. The full text is available at that link.

We are committed to a friendly, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for
everyone taking part in this project, regardless of background or identity.

## In short

- Be respectful and considerate in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Assume good intent, and focus on what is best for the project and community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, please report it
privately to the maintainer at **emailofsrijan@gmail.com**. All reports will be
reviewed and handled confidentially.
